BACKGROUND

I was born and raised in southern Alberta, Canada, as the second child of six and my family's oldest son. While growing up, I felt constant pressure to be a positive example for my younger siblings, which heavily influenced my decision to attend Brigham Young University.



ART

Many years ago I discovered my love for art. Even from an early age I was fascinated by the idea that I can take in my surroundings and portray my own perspective of them. Through sketching, drawing, and carving I can show my creative side and find real satisfaction in the work I do.
